About Me

I'm Salehin Ashfi, a writer, cybersecurity researcher, and developer exploring the intersection of technology and storytelling. My journey began at 15 when I turned to writing to navigate emotions and ideas, evolving into a passion for crafting immersive sci-fi narratives that challenge perceptions of time, existence, and the human psyche.

As the author of ChronoGenesis: The Shattered Future and its upcoming sequel ChronoGenesis: Echoes of the Architect, I weave intricate worlds where mystery, science, and psychological depth collide. My stories are driven by personal introspection, exploring the fragility of reality while delivering cinematic, thought-provoking experiences.

Beyond writing, I specialize in cybersecurity and ethical hacking, identifying vulnerabilities in major platforms, including Meta's bug bounty program. My work focuses on web security, exploit development, and system defense, ensuring a safer digital landscape. I also develop open-source projects and automation tools, continuously pushing the boundaries of security and innovation.
